The Cipher of Shadows

Only the clever may proceed. Five veiled riddles hold letters that forge the password — a word that heals.

1. The Silent Hunter
It sleeps by day in libraries of bark,
Eyes like moons, it reigns after dark.

2. The Ancient Pulse
It carries time upon its rings,
Roots deep in silence, yet it sings.

3. The Thief of Light
No door it enters, yet steals the scene,
Where it goes, all turns unseen.

4. The Breathless Dancer
It twists and sways but has no form,
Born from heat, yet never warm.

5. The Eternal Enigma
Always ahead, never behind,
The future's secret in its bind.
